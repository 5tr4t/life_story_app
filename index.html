The Complete Journey

1. Establishing the Arc

We start by asking a few foundational questions upfront to determine the overarching story arc and approve an outline.

2. Chapter-by-Chapter Discovery

You answer specific questions for each chapter of your life. During this phase, you can even invite others (like family or friends) to contribute their own perspectives.

3. Deepening the Narrative

As you go through the questions, our intelligent scribe asks gentle clarification questions where needed to ensure no precious detail is lost.

4. Drafting & Review

The chapters are beautifully drafted. You review the text, provide your feedback, and finalize the wording so it sounds exactly like you.

5. The Final Masterpiece

Once we get your final sign-off, the memoir is complete, sent to the press, and your physical book(s) is(are) printed and shipped.
